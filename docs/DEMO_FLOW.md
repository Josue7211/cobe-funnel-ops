# Demo Flow

## Goal

Walk through the system like an operator showing how the workflow reduces manual work and protects revenue.

## Path 1

### Hot lead to checkout

1. Open the DM funnel section.
2. Show the inbound pricing message.
3. Point out intent score, tags, source, and next action.
4. Show that the checkout handoff and event log update in the same flow.

## Path 2

### Booked call to recovery

1. Open the routing and recovery section.
2. Show the booked lead, reminder state, and no-show state.
3. Point out the automated recovery action and owner routing.

## Path 3

### Reporting and tracking

1. Open the dashboard section.
2. Show influenced revenue, show rate, and recovery rate.
3. End on the server-event naming and audit log to reinforce tracking discipline.
