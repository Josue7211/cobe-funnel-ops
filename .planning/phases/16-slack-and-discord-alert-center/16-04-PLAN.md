# 16-04: Link Alerts Back to the Workflow

## Goal
Make alerts part of the product workflow, not a side surface.

## Depends on
- 16-01 to 16-03

## Requirements
- ALERT-01, ALERT-02

## Success Criteria
1. Alerts link back to the lead lifecycle and queue.
2. Alert actions change visible state.

## Plan
- Connect alerts to workflow state.
- Update the linked lead or queue when needed.
- Keep the path back to the source clear.
