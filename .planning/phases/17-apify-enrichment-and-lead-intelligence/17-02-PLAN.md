# 17-02: Feed Enrichment Into Prioritization

## Goal
Make enrichment change score, route, or queue priority.

## Depends on
- 17-01

## Requirements
- ENRICH-01

## Success Criteria
1. Enrichment visibly affects routing or priority.
2. The operator can see the impact.

## Plan
- Map enrichment to priority signals.
- Update the lead record.
- Keep the change explainable.
